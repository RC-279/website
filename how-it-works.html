At Rocket Health, we put patients first

Mental & Sexual healthcare has been highly stigmatised in India. At Rocket Health, our aim is to create a judgement-free, inclusive space where patients can discuss and seek treatment from registered doctors and avail discreet delivery of drugs from the comfort of their home.

Online Visit

Report your symptoms and history to a registered doctor for evaluation.

Speak with a doctor

Connect to a doctor or therapist on text, phone, video depending on the condition.

Delivery of medication

(Not applicable for therapy)
If prescribed, your treatment would be sent in a discreet packaging and reach you within a week

Ongoing Care

Send your doctor/care team a message anytime for any queries related to your condition.

Technology that empowers, not replaces doctors
